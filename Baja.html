The Baja Buckeyes are The Ohio State University’s student engineering team that designs, builds, and races off-road vehicles in SAE Baja competitions, combining hands-on engineering, teamwork, and real-world problem solving.

As part of the team, I work on data acquisition, electrical design, and testing systems that bring our cars to life in competition.

My Role on the Team

As Data Acquisition Lead, I design and implement the systems that monitor our car’s performance in real time. This includes integrating sensors for suspension travel, engine data, GPS, and acceleration, as well as developing custom PCBs for data logging and CAN bus communication.

Through this work I’ve gained experience in electrical engineering, embedded systems, and testing under real competition conditions. Baja has strengthened my leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving skills, while giving me the opportunity to apply classroom knowledge to real-world engineering challenges.
