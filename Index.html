    80 Members | 20 Leaders | 10 Subteams | 10+ Majors | 1st Place in 2024

    Formula Buckeyes is the Formula SAE team of The Ohio State University. Our ten comprehensive sub teams made up entirely by students, design, manufacture, and compete a formula-style race car at both domestic and international competitions. As one of the top internal combustion teams in the US–recently highlighted by our 1st place finishes at Formula SAE Michigan, the Oakland Grand Prix, and the Pittsburgh Shootout–we are constantly pushing the boundaries of what is possible in collegiate motorsports and beyond.
