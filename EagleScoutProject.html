My Eagle Scout Project

Gaga Ball Pit at Heritage Park

Completed in October 2022, this project involved the design, planning, and construction of a permanent Gaga Ball Pit at Heritage Park in Versailles, Ohio.

Project Overview

As part of my journey to earning the rank of Eagle Scout, I designed and led the construction of a Gaga Ball Pit at Heritage Park. This community-centered project involved over 100 hours of planning, coordination, fundraising, and hands-on building.

The completed Gaga Ball Pit is now enjoyed by many families and children in the community, encouraging outdoor activity and play. The project taught me leadership, time management, and the importance of giving back.

Special Thanks

Thank you to the Versailles Parks Department, my Scout leaders, friends, family, and local sponsors who donated materials and time to make this project possible.
